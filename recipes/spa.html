German Spätzle

A South German Dream

Spätzle are South Germany's answer to Noodles, made from just Eggs,
Flour and a pinch of Salt, they fit almost every german Dish perfectly.
They are cheap, easy to make and taste sooo good!

Ingredients

  • 210g Flour
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Water
  • A pinch of Salt
  • A tool called a Spätzle Press is needed
    to squeeze the Dough into the water.

Steps

  1. Bring a Pot of water to boil, (don't forget to salt the water).
  2. In the meantime mix all ingredients thoroughly until they turn into a sticky dough.
  3. Put some of the dough into the Press (don't fill it up to much).
  4. When the Water is Boiling press the Dough into the Water.
  5. When the Water starts to foam take the spätzle out of the water with a sieve and place them in a Bowl.
  6. Repeat until done and enjoy your Spätzle, either just like that, or as a side dish with a delicious german meal.
