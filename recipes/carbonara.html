Carbonara

The Modern Italian Classic

With just a few simple ingredients, and neither much time nor money,
you can create one of the best and fulfilling Italian Dishes, out there!

Ingredients

  • 250g Spaghetti
  • 100g Bacon
  • 2 Eggs
  • 200ml Cream
  • 80g Parmesan
  • Salt, Pepper, Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Cook Spaghetti al dente.
  2. In the meantime cut the Bacon and cook it slightly crispy in a big Pan.
  3. Mix eggs, cream and the parmesan inside a bowl, then add some salt, pepper and nutmeg to the mix.
  4. Lastly put the Noodles directly from the boiling Water into the Pan, (make sure the Pan is not to hot),
    and add the Egg mix, then combine everything under low heat and serve at the Table.
